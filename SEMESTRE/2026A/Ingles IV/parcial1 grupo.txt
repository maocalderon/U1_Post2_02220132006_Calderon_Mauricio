Julián: Mesero (Waiter)

Andrés: Cliente 1

Josué: Cliente 2

Mauricio: Cliente 3

Escena 1: La llegada
Julián: Good afternoon! Welcome to our restaurant. How many people?
Andrés: Hello! We are three people, please.
Mauricio: Can I see the menu?
Julián: Sure! Here you go. (Entrega los menús a los tres). Today we have many special dishes.

Escena 2: Pidiendo la comida
Julián: Are you ready to order?
Andrés: Yes. I want some pasta with a few meatballs and a little parmesan cheese, please.
Josué: I want a burger with many onions, a lot of cheese, and a few pickles.
Julián: Great choice. And for you, Mauricio?
Mauricio: I want a salad with many tomatoes and a little salt.
Julián: Perfect. Anything to drink?
Andrés: I want a soda with much ice.
Josué: I want a beer with a little lemon.
Mauricio: A juice with a little sugar, please.
Julián: Excellent. I’ll be back in a few minutes with your drinks and food.

Escena 3: Interacción durante la comida
(Simulan que reciben la comida y prueban un bocado)
Andrés: This pasta is delicious! It has much flavor.
Josué: My burger is very big. It has too many onions, but it's good.
Mauricio: My salad is fresh. I like it!

Escena 4: El postre y la cuenta
Julián: Is everything okay? Do you want some dessert?
Andrés: No, thank you. I am very full!
Josué: Me too, the food was great.
Mauricio: Julián, the bill, please?
Julián: Yes, sir. Here is the check. The total is 75 dollars.
Andrés: Here you go. Thank you very much. (Simula pagar).
Mauricio: Goodbye!
Josué: See you later!
Julián: Have a nice day! Bye.
